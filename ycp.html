Our Values

Brief History

Since the bird called Nigeria learned to fly, our political nests have been overly dominated by the oldsters who barely recognize the presence and usefulness of the younger generation. Certainly, there won't be a change in this long known tradition until the youths of our dear Nigeria spread their wings and stand up for what they believe in, to fulfill the old notion of being "the future of Nigeria" so, the Youth Coalition Party (YCP).

YCP, the hub for politically conscious Nigerian Youths, was founded on the 30th of November, 2020. Following the #EndSARS protest that sparked the need for an active representation of the youth in government. The Nigerian youths have been cared-less for; in government's decisions, policies and plan, talents have been overlooked, development deprived, and our voices not heard; these and many others have been crystal clear over the years in all levels of administration.

Even though we represent the majority during the campaign and casting votes, we are relegated and don't matter to many political elites when they are in power. And here we are, tired-and-worn out. Enough of whimpering! It is time to take matters into our hands; hence united Nigerian youths have come under the wings of YCP from all states and tribes, intending to revitalize and restructure Nigeria's political and administrative systems.

Vision

  • Bring into power: disciplined leaders with distinguishable integrity.

  • Support and promote technological innovations.

  • Change the country’s “rich man's constitution" to the Constitution of the people.

  • Embrace accountability.

  • Uphold the principles of social contract and equality before the law.

Mission

  • Establish a healthy environment of interaction between the government and the people, especially the youths.

  • Have a policy for at least 50% inclusion of youths in political offices.

  • Ensure that the Judiciary and the Electoral Commission have true independence.

  • Revamp the educational system, the civil and public service.

Who we are

As a general rule, a government elected into office should seek to please the people that elected them to power. However, an exception to this rule has become a norm in Nigeria today, and this should not be. It is with an intent to change this norm that we have come together to attempt dislodging the previous narrative. We make our stand under the political party: Youth Coalition Party. We believe that nothing is impossible if we can put ourselves to work in unity while dedicating ourselves to discipline in service.
